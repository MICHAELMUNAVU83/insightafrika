About us

Insights Afrika Lab is an impact-driven firm founded to strengthen data-informed decision-making for our clients. We are Global South Led and operate through regional "lab" hubs, enabling close partnerships with global and local stakeholders and rapid on-the-ground support.

Where We Are
Our diverse team of evaluators brings experience from engagements with Civil Society Organisations ( CSO's ), Corporates, Established and Startup Social Enterprises, Multilateral agencies, Bilateral donors, Foundations, Governments , UN Agencies , INGOs and NGOs.

MEL Frameworks & Systems Design

MEL Frameworks & Systems Design

Designing comprehensive monitoring and evaluation frameworks from scratch or strengthening existing systems. We develop Theories of Change, logical frameworks, indicator sets, and data collection systems that align with donor requirements and national MIS standards.

Evaluations & Research

Evaluations & Research

Conducting independent evaluations across the project lifecycle including baseline studies, mid-term reviews, final evaluations, and impact assessments . We also undertake applied research, political economy analyses, needs assessments, and sectoral studies to inform policy and strategy.

Data Collection & Analytics

Data Collection & Analytics

End-to-end data collection and analysis services using digital tools, participatory methods, and rigorous statistical analysis to provide solid evidence for decision-making. We coordinate complex multi-country evaluations across Africa leveraging our pan-African presence and regional expertise.

Learning & Capacity Building

Learning & Capacity Building

Going beyond measurement to actively facilitate organizational learning through reflection workshops, training programs, and mentorship initiatives. We build sustainable MEL capacity within organizations to ensure long-term impact and continuous improvement.
